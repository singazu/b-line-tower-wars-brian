# Line Tower Wars App Review Notes

## App Summary

Line Tower Wars is a self-contained single-player lane defense strategy game. Players place towers, queue attackers, and compete across a three-round match against an AI opponent. The app includes persistent local progression with career records, win streak tracking, and lifetime attacker/tower performance.

## Reviewer Guidance

- No login or account is required.
- No external website is required for core functionality.
- The full match loop works offline after install.
- Progress is stored locally on-device.

## Core Gameplay Loop

1. Start a match from the title screen.
2. During prep, place towers in the five player slots.
3. Queue attackers for the upcoming wave.
4. Start the battle and watch both sides score as units cross the lane.
5. Between rounds, upgrade a tower or attacker card in the shop.
6. Complete the three-round match, then review the post-match battle report and persistent records.

## Persistent Value

- Career record board with matches played, wins, best win streak, and win rate
- Lifetime attacker scoring totals
- Lifetime tower elimination totals
- Milestones that encourage replay over multiple sessions

## Suggested Screenshot / Preview Coverage
