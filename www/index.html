How To Play

Win the lane in ten rounds

1. Build defense

Tap a tower card, then tap one of your five slots during prep. Stronger towers cost more mana.

2. Send pressure

Queue attackers into the next wave to score when they cross the lane before the AI stops them.

3. Upgrade smart

After each battle, spend mana in the round shop to improve future towers or attacker cards.
